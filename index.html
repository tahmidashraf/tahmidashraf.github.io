02 — Research Interests

Research Interests

My work sits at the intersection of distributed computing, machine learning, and real-world systems — with a strong focus on trustworthy, scalable, and intelligent technologies.

🔗
Federated Learning & Privacy
Designing fault-tolerant, auditable, and adaptive federated learning frameworks that address Byzantine behavior, heterogeneous client connectivity, and staleness under real-world conditions.
⛓
Blockchain & Decentralized Systems
Advancing blockchain-based tokenization frameworks for real estate, developing conceptual models for autonomous vehicle safety, and exploring blockchain-IoE convergence for security.
🤖
LLM-Guided Reinforcement Learning
Leveraging large language models to guide population-based reinforcement learning for adaptive hyperparameter optimization and intelligent agent design in AR/VR educational settings.
🥽
AI for Education (AR/VR)
Applying cross-dataset machine learning to predict learning outcomes from AR/VR data, and building RL frameworks to adapt educational content delivery in immersive environments.
🚗
Autonomous & Safety-Critical Systems
Integrating blockchain-enabled federated learning for collaborative autonomous vehicle navigation and developing real-time collision prevention frameworks using smart contracts.
📡
Next-Generation Networks
Exploring meta-learning approaches such as FedFOMAML for next-beam prediction in mmWave networks, addressing the intersection of communication systems and distributed intelligence.
🌐
IoE Security & Smart Contracts
Investigating the convergence of blockchain, the Internet of Everything (IoE), and federated learning for building secure, decentralized infrastructure — including smart contract-driven access control and trust mechanisms across connected devices.
🌿
ML for Environmental & Industrial Sensing
Applying machine learning to assess data quality from distributed acoustic sensing (DAS) systems for critical applications such as CO₂ leak detection — bridging signal processing, anomaly detection, and environmental safety.
